How whiteboard works

The whiteboard button inserts a structured markdown template inside your note.

  • Node: the idea or section you are mapping.
  • Linked doc: file, URL, or note tied to that node.
  • Citation: page/section evidence for traceability.
  • Open question: unresolved item to revisit.

For a full drag-and-link canvas, the next step is a dedicated Board panel with movable cards, connection lines, and inline comments that sync back to notes.
